# Product Brief

**Summary**: The first artifact in the [[question-chain]], covering Q1–Q3: what is being built, who the stakeholders are, and who the customers are.

**Sources**: raw/question-chain-v2.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-18

---

**Owner:** PM-A with Product Director

## Q1: What are we building and why?

Answer format: a single sentence — "We are building _____ so that _____."

If different people give different answers, alignment is missing before any work begins. This is the first red flag check in the [[question-chain]].

## Q2: Who cares about results but won't use the product directly?

These are stakeholders — roles like compliance officer, finance director, or client relationship manager. They care about outcomes (accuracy, timeliness, compliance) but won't log in.

Answer format: a list of role titles.

## Q3: Who is our customer?

External people or organizations who receive value from the product. Roles, not individual names — e.g. client settlement operator, fund manager.

Answer format: a list of customer role titles.

These customer roles reappear in Q5 and Q6 ([[user-journeys]]).
